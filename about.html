About Us

PTE Core Practice is dedicated to helping students prepare for the Pearson Test of English (PTE) exam. Our platform offers comprehensive practice materials for all sections of the test.

Our team consists of experienced language teachers and PTE experts who have designed these practice materials to closely match the actual exam format and difficulty level.

We focus on the core components of the PTE exam, including Repeat Sentence (RS) and Write From Dictation (WFD), which are crucial for achieving a high score.
